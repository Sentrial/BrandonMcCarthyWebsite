PROJECTS

    Can you save the damsel in distress before it's too late?

  • Distressed 2

    IN PROGRESS


    Distressed2 is a remake and redesign of the orignal Distressed with more mechanics, puzzles, art, and secrets. Uncover the mystery and solve the puzzle in this dual ending 3D story / puzzle game!

  • Floor After Floor

    IN PROGRESS


    You find yourself in an abandoned mall with one goal:
    Make money and get out.
    Fight your way through each floor, loot stores, and find the extraction point.

    Floor After Floor was delevoped in a group of 5 in a Games course at the University of Utah where I served as the vision holder contributing to blueprinting, modeling, rigging, animations, level design, and UI design.
